The people involved in this article are Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, popularly known today as Mahatma Gandhi and British official Lord Mountbatten. The article is about the Indian Independence Movement, The struggle of the Indian people for Independence from the British people. This article is newsworthy because it describes the basic process of independence and the issues that came after achieving independence This event took place on august 14 1947 for Pakistan and 15 August 1947 for India. This event took place in the British Parliament(where the bill was passed) and in the countries of India and Pakistan. Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was the leader of the Indian National Congress, a nationalist and anti-britan(ruling) party. He led salt marches protesting the ban of procuring salt from anyone besides the British Nation. He went to the ocean along with a lot of supporters and extracted salt from the ocean. After the Indian Country was split into Pakistan and India, there was a lot of violence due to the division and the line that now divided the sate of Punjab, West Punjab in Pakistan, East Punjab in India. Along the India-Pakistan border, violence was prominent and even people who were supposed to be law enforcement contributed to the violence.
